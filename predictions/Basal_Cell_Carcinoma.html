Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is basal cell carcinoma the most common type of skin cancer?

A: Yes, basal cell carcinoma is the most common type of skin cancer, accounting for the majority of cases.

Q: Can basal cell carcinoma spread to other parts of the body?

A: While it's rare, basal cell carcinoma can spread to nearby tissues if left untreated. Early detection is key for effective treatment.

Q: What are the risk factors for developing basal cell carcinoma?

A: Risk factors include prolonged sun exposure, fair skin, a history of sunburns, and a family history of skin cancer.
