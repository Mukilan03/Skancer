Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What are the warning signs of Melanoma?

A: Warning signs include changes in the size, shape, color, or elevation of a mole. Itching, tenderness, or bleeding can also be indicators.

Q: Is Melanoma a common type of skin cancer?

A: Melanoma is less common than other types of skin cancer, but it is more aggressive. Early detection is crucial for successful treatment.

Q: What are the risk factors for developing Melanoma?

A: Risk factors include a history of sunburns, excessive UV exposure, fair skin, a family history of Melanoma, and the presence of many moles.
